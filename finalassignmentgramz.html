A small snip-it of a poem I recently wrote!

Every quiet night
The sunsets are slow
Letting time awaken the moon
And all the little stars in the sky
They sparkle and dance
In the everlasting Sun’s light
While planets rotate around the sun
The stars themselves
Are cast out
they're all forgotten
Except for one
The Northern Star....
